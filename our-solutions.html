Our Solutions

Step One

A free quotation
is needed to assess the clients bandwidth needs and location.

Step Two

A dedicated team of surveyors will determine the best location to install the Wireless Access Broadband equipment on a client’s premises.

Step Three

The third step is the installation process. Completion is achieved in a maximum of 14 days.
It will include setting up a point-to-point wireless link from the antenna on a client’s premises to our nearest Wireless Access Broadband main antenna, providing a guaranteed strong and stable signal connection.

Step Four

The final step is the connection process. A team of engineers will help the client to connect their wireless-compatible devices to an Access Point, allowing a lightning- fast, state-of-the-art network.

How Is It Provided

Using a systematic approach of setting up antennas and Wireless Access Broadband, in conjunction with the New York City Housing Authority, coverage will be extended throughout New York within tightly knit deadlines.
XMG has a timeline for completion in approximately 12 months. ​ This leading-edge, free and secure wireless connectivity solution from XMG is the future of networking and internet access as a whole. It offers a variety of benefits from scalability, flexible bandwidth speeds, dedicated services across the Ethernet, MPLS, VPN and Private Lines. Having the tallest building in the United States - One World Trade Center, as the main center of antennas in the region, this will be at the very heart of the point-to-point mesh.
